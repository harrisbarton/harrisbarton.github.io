Geometric Deep Learning

About

  • Here I will post some notes on an interesting Paper introducing Geometric Deep Learning. This paper introduces the concept with an incredibly interesting goal: "obtaining a systematisation of Deep Learning and ‘connecting the dots’". They "propose to derive different inductive biases and network architectures implementing them from first principles of symmetry and invariance". I find this fascinating and want to investigate this field further throughout this semester.
